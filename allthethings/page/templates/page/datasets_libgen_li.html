Datasets ▶ Libgen.li

For the backstory of the different Library Genesis forks, see the page for the Libgen.rs.

The Libgen.li contains most of the same content and metadata as the Libgen.rs, but has some collections on top of this, namely comics, magazines, and standard documents. It has also integrated Sci-Hub into its metadata and search engine (see Libgen.rs for more information).

The metadata for this library is freely available. However, there are no torrents available for the additional content. The torrents that are on the Libgen.li website are mirrors of other torrents listed here.
